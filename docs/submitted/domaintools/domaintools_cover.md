I am also eager to learn; Rust is on my list and this role's use of it alongside Go is a strong draw. What excites me most about DomainTools is the chance to apply everything I have built - security infrastructure, high volume data systems, web platform expertise, and SRE rigor - toward making the Internet safer for everyone. Integrity, teamwork, innovation, and joy, grounded in the autonomy to tackle challenges your own way. That is exactly how I work best.
